Welcome to

Beatmakers Lair

Lex Gxn (Alex Oganesyan) is a young producer from Yerevan. His beats consist of warm and lush instrumentation while keeping a raw and authentic feeling through his obscure samples. He is heavily influenced by producers like Southside, Metro Boomin, ATL Jacob and DJ Tape.

He started writing music thanks to his friend who once showed him how FL Studio works. This inspired him very much, and he started working: he tried, experimented, found his own sound. And at the moment he can show you the fruits of his labors.
